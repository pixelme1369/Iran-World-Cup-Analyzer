The match that decides everything

Egypt vs Iran, June 26 — Seattle

This is the only fixture Iran controls directly, and it caps their ceiling. A loss locks Iran on 2 points for the tournament — mathematically out of 1st place no matter what happens in New Zealand vs Belgium, since Egypt would already be sitting on 4+ points from the win alone.

A draw keeps Iran alive for 2nd or a third-place slot, but only a win opens the door to topping the group, and even then it likely comes down to goal difference or goals scored against Belgium.
